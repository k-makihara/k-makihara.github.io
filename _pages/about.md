Dr. Koshi Makihara is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Embodied AI Research Team, National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST), Tokyo, Japan. 

- 2021 - 2025 Ph.D student at [Robotics Manipulation Lab](https://www.roboticmanipulation.org/english/), Graduate School of Science and Engineering, The University of Osaka (Supervisor: Prof. Kensuke Harada).
- 2019 - 2021 Master's student at [Manipulation System Lab](https://sites.google.com/ms.esys.tsukuba.ac.jp/www/home?authuser=0), Graduate School of System and Information Engineering, The University of Tsukuba (Supervisor: Prof. Yasumichi Aiyama).
- 2016 - 2019 Bachelor's student at [Manipulation Robotics Lab](https://www.fit.ac.jp/~makita/index-en.html), National Institute of Technology, Sasebo College (Supervisor: Associate Prof. Satoshi Makita).

Research Interests
======
My major interest is the smart warehouse utilizing robotic arms: visual recognition and grasping, as well as action planning and generation for next-generation logistics.
